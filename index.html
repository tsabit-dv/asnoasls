Personnel On Board (POB) Tracking Systems

In security, it is critical to know where workers are at all times for safety, compliance and emergency response. The Personnel On Board (POB) Tracking System allows users to efficiently monitor and control the movement of personnel without the need for manual effort and facilitates faster emergency evacuations. This allows personnel to be tracked in real time; allow people to be stationed during emergencies; and also define access and denial of entry to hazardous areas such as confined spaces.
